TRADE PROMOTION AGREEMENT — END-CAP DISPLAY
Brand: Mondelez International ("Brand")  |  Retailer: Walmart Supercenter #2643 ("Retailer")
Promotion: Q2 OREO End-Cap Feature  |  Agreement No. WMT-OREO-Q2-2026

SECTION 1. TERM
1.1  The promotional display shall be installed and continuously maintained from
     May 1, 2026 through May 31, 2026 inclusive (the "Promotion Window").

SECTION 2. EXECUTION REQUIREMENTS
2.1  Placement. The feature shall occupy a full end-cap at the head of the cookie /
     cracker aisle. Aisle-side or in-line shelf placement does not satisfy this Section.
2.2  Facings. A minimum of four (4) product facings of OREO (any SKU) shall be
     presented across the end-cap.
2.3  Branding. The end-cap shall display the official OREO branded header card with
     the OREO wordmark clearly legible.
2.4  Stock. The display shall be reasonably stocked (no predominantly empty shelves)
     for the duration of the Promotion Window.

SECTION 3. PROOF OF PERFORMANCE
3.1  Retailer shall submit at least one in-store photograph of the installed end-cap
     together with a short narrative attesting to compliance with Section 2.

SECTION 4. SETTLEMENT
4.1  Upon verified compliance, Brand shall pay Retailer a promotion fee of up to
     30 HBAR (testnet demonstration denomination).
4.2  Partial compliance may be settled at a proportionate credit at Brand's reasonable
     determination.
