CO-OPERATIVE MARKETING SCHEDULE — SEASONAL FLOOR DISPLAY
Between Mondelez International (the "Supplier") and Target Corporation (the "Merchant")
Re: Cadbury Creme Egg — Easter 2026 Freestanding Display
Schedule reference: TGT/CADB/EAST-2026

(a)  Display window. The freestanding display must be live on the sales floor for the
     full Easter selling period, concluding end of day April 20, 2026. Settlement is
     conditional on the Merchant demonstrating the display was live within this window.

(b)  Location. A freestanding display unit (FSDU) positioned in a front-of-store or
     primary entrance zone. Standard gondola/in-aisle shelving is excluded.

(c)  Product presentation. No fewer than six (6) facings of Cadbury Creme Egg on the
     unit.

(d)  Theming. Easter-themed branded signage bearing the Cadbury mark must be affixed
     to the unit.

(e)  Evidence. The Merchant shall provide a dated in-store photograph and a brief
     narrative. Where the photograph alone does not establish that the display was live
     within the window in (a) — for example where no date is visible — the Supplier may
     request supplementary proof of timing (e.g. a point-of-sale timestamp or a dated
     compliance report) before settling.

(f)  Consideration. Subject to verification, the Supplier shall remit a co-operative
     marketing fee of up to 25 HBAR (testnet demonstration denomination), or a
     proportionate amount for partial fulfilment.
